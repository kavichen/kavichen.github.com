在MacVim 7.3下，无法设置透明度

  • Transparency should definitely work with the experimental renderer: go into the advanced prefs, enable the experimental renderer, the quit MacVim and restart, try ":set transp=50". If it doesn't work, then go through the steps in the ReportingBugs wiki page [1]. (If you reset the preferences as outline there you'll have to go into the advanced prefs and re-enable the experimental renderer afterwards.)
